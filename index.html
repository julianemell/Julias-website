About me.

Currently studying at Medieinstitutet in Malmö to be a Front End Developer. I have a previous career as in-house sales within the retail apparel business. I have a Bachelor in Textile Management and Business Administration from the Swedish School of Textiles in Borås.

Projects

Almi

A project that focused on design for a new (made up) campaign for Almi. The wireframe was created in Adobe XD, the photos edited in Photoshop and the design assets were made in Illustrator - all inspired or copied from Almi’s visual identity.

The project will help me as a Front End Developer to better communicate with the designers in my (future) team through the knowledge of how the web designer's process works.
